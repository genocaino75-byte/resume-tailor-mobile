Help Center

Answers to common questions about using Resume Tailored to JD.

How does resume tailoring work?

Paste your resume and a job description into the Tailor screen. Our AI (powered by Claude) analyzes both and rewrites your resume to better highlight the skills and experience most relevant to that specific role.

Is my resume data private?

Yes. Your resume and job description text is only used to generate your tailored resume, and is never sold or used for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for full details on how your data is handled.

How do I save a tailored resume?

After tailoring, tap the "Save" button on the results screen. You can view all your saved resumes anytime in the History tab.

How do I download my resume?

On the results screen, tap "Export" and choose either Word (.docx) or PDF format.

How do I delete a saved resume?

Go to the History tab, and tap the trash icon on any saved resume card.

How do I delete my entire account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account." This permanently removes your account and all saved resumes, and cannot be undone.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your account email. We'll send you a reset link — open it, choose a new password, and then log in with your new password in the app. If the email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder or reach out through Contact Us.

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app first. If the issue continues, reach out through Contact Us with details about what happened, and we'll look into it.
