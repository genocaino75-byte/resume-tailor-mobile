Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Resume Tailored to JD ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. Resume Tailored to JD is developed and operated by Paul Cain, an individual developer.

If you have questions about this policy, contact: support@cainbuilds.com

1. Information We Collect

When you use Resume Tailored to JD, we collect:

  • Account information: your email address and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store your actual password in readable form).
  • Resume and job description content: the text of resumes and job descriptions you paste into the app, and the AI-tailored resumes generated from them.
  • Usage data: basic technical information such as request timestamps, needed to operate the app securely.

We do not collect payment information, location data, contacts, photos, or device identifiers beyond what's needed for basic app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Create and authenticate your account
  • Generate AI-tailored resumes based on the resume and job description text you provide
  • Save your resume history so you can access it later
  • Maintain and improve the security and functionality of the app

3. Third-Party Services

To provide the resume-tailoring feature, the text you submit (your resume and the job description) is sent to Anthropic (the maker of Claude AI) for processing. Anthropic processes this data to generate your tailored resume and does not use it to train their models under their standard API terms. See Anthropic's own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy for details on how they handle this data.

Your account data and saved resumes are stored using Render (render.com), our hosting and database provider. Render stores this data on our behalf and does not use it for any purpose other than providing hosting services to us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt hashing before storage — we cannot see or recover your actual password.
  • Login sessions are managed using secure, time-limited authentication tokens (JWT).
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Saved resumes remain in your account until you delete them yourself using the delete option in the app's History screen.
  • You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data at any time using the Delete Account option in the app's Settings screen. This immediately and permanently removes your account information and all saved resumes — this action cannot be undone.
  • If you're unable to access the app but would like your account deleted, you may also contact us at support@cainbuilds.com to request deletion.

6. Children's Privacy

Resume Tailored to JD is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can exercise these rights by:

  • Deleting individual saved resumes directly in the app's History screen
  • Permanently deleting your entire account and all associated data using the Delete Account option in the app's Settings screen
  • Contacting support@cainbuilds.com with any questions about your data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
